# JustCall

**Reliable international video calling — no apps, no accounts, no hassle.**

[**www.just-call.app**](https://just-call.app)

## The problem

Calling someone in another country over video shouldn't be complicated — but it often is. Apps require downloads. Services demand sign-ups. Connections drop behind firewalls and restrictive networks. You just want to talk to someone, and the tooling gets in the way.

## How JustCall works

1. Open the app and start a call.
2. Send the link to whoever you want to talk to.
3. They click it. You're connected.

That's it. No installs, no accounts, no app stores. It runs entirely in the browser.

## Why it works internationally

Most video call solutions struggle when participants are in different countries — especially behind corporate firewalls or restrictive NATs. JustCall is built on WebRTC with a relay architecture designed specifically for these situations. Connections route through the best available path automatically, so calls go through even when other tools fail.

## What you get

- **Instant access** — no sign-up, no download, just a link
- **Cross-border reliability** — connects across countries and difficult networks
- **Clear video and audio** — adaptive bitrate keeps things smooth under varying conditions
- **Privacy by default** — media is end-to-end encrypted, nothing is stored on servers
- **Works everywhere** — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Brave — desktop or mobile

## Who it's for

- Families spread across countries who just want a quick call
- Freelancers and small teams who need a video chat without the overhead
- Anyone who's tired of asking "can you hear me?" on other platforms
- A reliable backup when your usual tool won't connect
